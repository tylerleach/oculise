The Oculise Project

Our goal is to create a navigable representation of real world cities in virtual reality so that you can explore new locations without physically being there. Furthermore we wish to explore and test the possible uses of virtual reality as it is a fairly new technology.

The Client: Nearmap

Nearmap is an aerial imagery technology and location data company that provides high resolution aerial maps, and city-scale 3D data sets. Nearmap also provides access to their imagery and geospatial tools on all devices such as web, mobile, tablet or desktop by making use of cloud based computing which allows users to make location analysis from their home or office without physically visiting the site/location.
